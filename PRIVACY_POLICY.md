# Privacy Policy for YouTube Music Coda Pack

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## Overview

YouTube Music Coda Pack ("the Pack", "we", "our") is a Coda Pack that integrates with YouTube Data API v3 to provide music management features within Coda documents.

## Information We Collect

### Information from YouTube API

When you connect your YouTube/Google account to this Pack, we access:

- **Your YouTube playlists** - To display and manage your playlists
- **Your liked videos** - To show your liked music videos
- **Your subscriptions** - To display channels you follow
- **Your channel information** - To show your profile data
- **Public video information** - To search and retrieve music data

### What We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ We do NOT store your Google password
- ❌ We do NOT collect personal identification beyond what YouTube API provides
- ❌ We do NOT track your activity outside of Coda
- ❌ We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT store your data on our servers

## How We Use Your Information

Your YouTube data is used ONLY to:

1. **Display your content** - Show your playlists, liked videos, and subscriptions in Coda
2. **Search YouTube** - Find music, channels, and playlists
3. **Manage playlists** - Create, modify, and organize your playlists
4. **Provide recommendations** - Use YouTube's API to suggest related content
5. **Show analytics** - Analyze playlist composition and statistics

## Data Storage

**All data remains in YOUR Coda workspace:**
- Data is stored in your Coda documents
- We do not maintain separate databases
- Data is subject to Coda's privacy policy
- When you delete data from Coda, it's deleted

**Authentication tokens:**
- Stored securely by Coda
- Used only to access YouTube API on your behalf
- Can be revoked at any time

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone except:

- **YouTube/Google** - To retrieve your data via their API
- **Coda** - Platform where the Pack runs (subject to Coda's privacy policy)

We do NOT:
- Sell your data
- Share with advertisers
- Provide to third-party analytics
- Use for marketing purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access your data** - View all data in your Coda documents
- **Delete your data** - Remove the Pack or delete Coda tables
- **Revoke access** - Disconnect your Google account at any time
- **Export your data** - Export from Coda in standard formats

## Revoking Access

To revoke this Pack's access to your YouTube data:

1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
2. Find "YouTube Music Coda Pack"
3. Click "Remove Access"

Or disconnect from within Coda:
1. Open your Coda doc
2. Find the Pack settings
3. Click "Disconnect"

## Children's Privacy

This Pack is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## API Usage and Quotas

This Pack uses YouTube Data API v3, which has usage quotas:
- Default: 10,000 units per day
- Shared across all users of this Pack
- Quota resets daily

## Data Security

Security measures:

- Encrypted API communications (HTTPS)
